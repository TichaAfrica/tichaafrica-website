Step 1 of 3 — What this actually is

Before you join, understand your role.

The Founding Teacher Alliance is not a programme you consume.

It is a working group of teachers contributing real insight into how AI should function in African classrooms.

You are not joining to be trained first.
You are joining because what you already know matters.

How the Alliance works

1. You share real classroom experiences
What works. What fails. What is missing.

2. We identify patterns across teachers
Not isolated stories; system-level insight.

3. Those insights shape what gets built
Tools, training, and systems grounded in reality.

4. You stay connected to the evolution
Not as a user; but as a contributor.

This is how teachers move from the edge of decisions to the centre of them.

What makes this different

Most platforms train you to use tools.

This platform uses your experience to shape them.

Most communities share ideas.

This one captures insight and turns it into direction.

Most systems are built first, then adapted later.

This starts with the classroom.

What happens after you join

• You enter a structured community of teachers

• You receive guided prompts to share real insights

• You see how your input connects to larger patterns

• You contribute to shaping what comes next

You do not need to be an expert in AI.

You need to be honest about your classroom.

That is where this begins.
