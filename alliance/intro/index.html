Step 1 of 3 — The Pledge

The Founding Teacher Pledge

Teacher, thank you for showing up.
This moment is bigger than any of us alone; but it starts with you.
You are the one who lives the classroom every day.
Your stories, your struggles, your victories; they’re the foundation we’ve been missing.

So take a breath. Read the pledge.
And if it feels right…
claim your place as one of Africa’s founding teachers.

I commit to shaping Africa’s learning future; not as a follower, but as a founder.

I will share the truth of my classroom so the tools we build reflect our realities, honor our languages, and lift our learners.

My voice, my experience, my wisdom; they belong here.

And together, we will make sure no foreign system ever decides for us again.

I have read and I affirm this pledge
