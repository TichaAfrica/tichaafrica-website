Step 1 of 3 — The Pledge

This is your moment, Teacher

Thank you for being here.
This is not a form. It is the beginning of something historical.

We have carried the classroom every day; through blackouts, chalk dust, endless marking, and dreams bigger than any syllabus allows.
Our wisdom is what has been missing at the table.

Read the pledge below.
If it feels true in your chest… claim it.

I commit to shaping Africa’s learning future — not as a follower, but as a founder.

I will share the truth of my classroom so the tools we build reflect our realities, honour our languages, and lift our learners.

My voice, my experience, my wisdom — they belong here.
And together, we will make sure no foreign system ever decides for us again.

I have read it. I affirm this pledge.
