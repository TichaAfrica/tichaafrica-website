🌍 The Founding Teacher Alliance

200 Teachers. One Continent. A Future Rewritten.

Why This Alliance Exists

Every day across Africa, teachers create miracles — with overcrowded classes, limited tools, and endless creativity. Yet, most AI tools are designed far away, without their voices.

The Founding Teacher Alliance flips the script. Here, teachers are not adapting to technology; they are designing its future.

Who We’re Looking For

  • 👩🏽‍🏫 Classroom teachers (rural & urban) bringing real, daily challenges and innovations
  • 🧑🏽‍💻 Digiteachers & EdTech developers shaping digital futures
  • 🏛️ Policy shapers & curriculum influencers ensuring impact reaches every school

What Founding Teachers Will Do

  • ✅ Align AI tools with African classroom realities
  • ✅ Test and refine early teaching apps and solutions
  • ✅ Mentor other teachers and ensure grassroots participation
  • ✅ Make learning more interactive, inclusive and creative
  • ✅ Co-create Africa’s first teacher-led AI curriculum, guided by UNESCO AI CFT and AU AI Strategy

What Founding Teachers Gain

  • 🌟 Recognition as one of only 200 Founding Teachers shaping Africa’s education future
  • 📜 Certification & visibility in global reports and platforms
  • 🛠️ Early access to new teaching tools and apps
  • 🤝 Connections with international partners, EdTech makers, and policymakers
  • 🏆 Legacy: remembered as a pioneer who redefined learning for millions

How It Works

This Alliance is powered quietly in the background by Ticha Africa — a teacher-led movement creating Africa’s first mobile-first AI training curriculum. But the spotlight is firmly on the teachers.
