Why TichaAfrica Exists

Every day, African teachers achieve the extraordinary—teaching overcrowded classes, with scarce resources, and little recognition. Yet, as AI reshapes global education, their voices are often missing from the table.

TichaAfrica exists to change that. We believe the future of learning must be built with teachers, not around them. By aligning with UNESCO’s AI Competency Framework for Teachers (AI CFT) and the African Union Continental AI Strategy, we bridge global innovation with African realities—turning teachers’ expertise into the driving force of AI in our classrooms.
