The problem

AI is arriving before teachers are prepared

If teachers are not first in the design room, decades of indigenous knowledge and classroom wisdom risk being ignored. That is a loss nobody can afford.

Imported design misses the classroom

Global AI tools are rarely built for African languages, resources or learning contexts. TichaAfrica puts teachers at the steering wheel.

The solution — led by teachers

Teacher-led curriculum

Curriculum co-created with teachers across the continent, mapped to the UNESCO AI Competency Framework for Teachers.

Mobile-first tools

Practical templates, lesson aids and micro-courses designed for low-bandwidth environments and everyday classroom use.

What founding teachers gain

  • Shape the continent’s first teacher co-created AI curriculum.
  • Receive a Founding Teacher badge & certificate.
  • Early access to tools, templates and micro-courses.
  • Participate in research and policy dialogues with partners.
