Why TichaAfrica Exists

African teachers achieve the extraordinary daily—teaching overcrowded classes with scarce resources and little recognition. Yet, as AI reshapes global education, their voices are often missing from the table.

TichaAfrica exists to change that. We align UNESCO’s AI Competency Framework for Teachers (AI CFT) with the African Union Continental AI Strategy, bridging global innovation with African realities—making teachers the driving force of AI in our classrooms.
