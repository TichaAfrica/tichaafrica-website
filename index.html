The Reality

AI is already entering our classrooms. If teachers are not in the room where AI is designed, decades of local knowledge — our languages, cultural practices and teaching methods — risk being overwritten. TichaAfrica makes sure teachers are not bystanders. We lead.

  • Language-first tools that respect local dialects and expression
  • Low-bandwidth delivery to reach every teacher, even without fast internet

For Your Career

  • Policy voice — opportunities to join research and policy conversations
  • Leadership opportunities on continental projects and research

A Direct Choice

AI will reach African classrooms — with or without teachers. Our choice now is whether it arrives shaped by our values, languages and judgement, or by systems that don't understand us. Join TichaAfrica to ensure AI protects our children and strengthens our teaching.
