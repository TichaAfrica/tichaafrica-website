The Problem

AI is arriving faster than teachers are prepared

If teachers are not in the design room, African classrooms risk adopting tools that do not reflect our realities or our cultures. TichaAfrica ensures teachers lead the transition responsibly.

Our Solution — Teacher-Led, African-Centred

  • Every learning module begins with real teacher insights.
  • Mobile-first, low-bandwidth delivery for every device.
  • Free foundational AI training for African teachers.
  • Mapped to UNESCO AI CFT and AU Continental AI Strategy.

What Founding Teachers Gain

  • Co-create Africa’s first teacher-driven AI curriculum.
  • Receive the Founding Teacher digital badge and certificate.
  • Exclusive early access to tools, templates, and micro-courses.
  • Opportunities to contribute to research and policy work.
