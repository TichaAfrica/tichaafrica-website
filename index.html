The real problem

This is not a skills problem.

A teacher managing 60 students, three languages, and no reliable internet; and still reaching the child in the back row; is not behind.

She is running adaptive systems that AI is still trying to replicate.

The gap is not in teacher capability.
The gap is in how AI is being designed.

Most AI conversations in African education start by asking what teachers need to learn. Nobody is asking what teachers already know that the tools haven't caught up with yet.
