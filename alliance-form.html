Welcome, Teacher.

You are joining a growing group of African educators contributing their professional experience to how artificial intelligence enters our classrooms.

This is not just a form. It is a teacher-led effort to ensure technology supports African learners, languages, and educational realities.

Teachers from Anglophone, Francophone, Lusophone, and Arabic-speaking education systems across Africa are welcome.
Your responses are used only for educational research, community dialogue, and programme design led by teachers.

There is no commercial use, no automated profiling, and no representation without consent.

Artificial intelligence will enter African classrooms — with or without teachers.
Your participation helps ensure it reflects African knowledge systems and protects learners.

Teach Smart. Lead Change.
